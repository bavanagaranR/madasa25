Event Details

Quiz’n Bugs Symposium is a coding challenge designed to test your debugging skills and logical reasoning. Compete against the best minds, crack code bugs, and showcase your coding expertise in an exciting, fast-paced event.

Time & Venue

08:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Panimalar Engineering College Chennai, India

Event Schedule

Participants are requested to check-in at the registration desk upon arrival. The debugging sessions will begin promptly at 08:00 AM.after then coding Round will be Schedule based on debugging round's results.

Participation Rules

Prelims (Online)

Conducted via Google Form with coding-related questions.
Top 10 scorers qualify for the final round.
No external assistance is allowed during the quiz.

Finals (Offline)

1. Debugging Section:
10 minutes to solve debugging questions.
10 minutes for evaluation and break.
2. Coding Round:
30 minutes on HackerRank to solve coding challenges.
General Rules:
Participants must adhere to the timeline; latecomers will not be allowed.
Computers and resources will be provided by the college.
Cheating or misconduct will result in immediate disqualification.
