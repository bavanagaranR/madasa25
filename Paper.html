Event

Paper Presentation is a premier event at our college symposium designed for students to showcase their research and innovative ideas in a competitive format. This event is a fantastic opportunity for students from various disciplines to present their findings and engage with fellow academics.

Time & Venue

08:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Panimalar Engineering College Chennai, India

Event Schedule

Participants are requested to check-in at the registration desk upon arrival. The presentation sessions will begin promptly at 08:00 AM. Each team will be allotted a specific time slot for their presentation..

Participation Rules

Prelims Rules (Online)

1. Submission: Submit an abstract (max 300 words) by the deadline via email.
2. Format: Use the provided abstract template: Click
3. Selection: Teams will be shortlisted based on their abstracts.
4. Results: The top 10 teams will be notified by email for the offline finals.
(Join the WhatsApp group for updates.)

Finals Rules

(Offline Presentation)

1. Submission: Submit the full paper and final PPT by email 48 hours before the presentation.
2. Time Limit: Presentations are limited to 6 minutes. No exceptions.
3. Visual Aids: Additional audiovisual aids are allowed but must be pre-approved.
4. Q&A: A Q&A will follow each presentation.
5. Judging: Judging will be based on content, clarity, delivery, and handling of questions.
(We will email the Paper format after the registration).
