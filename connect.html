Event

Connextverse is a high-energy competition where observation and quick thinking are your best allies!
Solve puzzles, connect images, and act out clues to win this ultimate test of wit and creativity.

Time & Venue

08:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Panimalar Engineering College Chennai, India

Event Schedule

Participants are requested to check-in at the registration desk upon arrival. The ConnextVerse sessions will begin promptly at 08:00 AM..

Participation Rules

Prelims Rules (Online)

* 25 questions, participants to answer.
* Points for correct answers and negative marks for wrong ones.

Finals (Offline )

1. Round 1: Image Connect
Identify the common link between images.
Fastest buzzer gets to answer.
Highest score moves to the final round.
2. Final Round: Act & Guess
Teams act out clues using gestures only (no sounds or props).
60 seconds per turn to guess the word or concept.
3. Fair Play & Tech Requirements
Stable internet required for online rounds.
Cheating or external help leads to disqualification.
4. Organizers' Verdict
All scoring and results are final and non-negotiable.
